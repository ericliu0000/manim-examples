Overview

This was created as part of participation in the Mentorship program during SRIP. Aerial photogrammetry, or the process of collecting 3d data about a certain cite from photos, can be accomplished using drones. In order to produce accurate results, a controlled amount of overlap must exist between each image. This video attempts to show this process (voiceover missing).
