Positive Triangle Game

Final project for MA4500: Graph Theory with REX Math
Eric L., Emmy T., Cooper X.

Overview

The Positive Triangle Game is a game in which players take turns marking edges on a graph. A positive triangle is a 3-cycle within that graph with zero negative signs and three positive signs, or two negative signs and one positive sign. The player which places down the mark creating a positive triangle wins the game.

There are a variety of play strategies that can be used in this game, leading to structural patterns in how many moves lead to a winning position, as well as a dominant strategy in the game. This is a video reviewing these aspects.
